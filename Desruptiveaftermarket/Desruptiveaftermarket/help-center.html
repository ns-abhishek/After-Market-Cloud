Frequently Asked Questions

View All arrow_forward
  • How do I reset my password? expand_more
    To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your email.
  • How do I add a new party? expand_more
    To add a new party, go to the Party List page and click on the "Add Party" button. Fill in the required information and click "Save".
  • How do I export data from the grid? expand_more
    To export data, click on the export button in the grid header. You can choose to export as CSV or Excel format.
  • How do I use the advanced search? expand_more
    Click on the advanced search button in the grid header. You can add multiple search criteria and use AND/OR operators to create complex queries.
