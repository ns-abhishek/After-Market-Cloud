Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about the ERP system. If you can't find what you're looking for, please contact our support team.

General Questions

What is an ERP system?

ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) is a software system that helps organizations manage and integrate their core business processes. It typically includes modules for finance, human resources, inventory management, customer relationship management, manufacturing, and more. The ERP system provides a central database that allows different departments to access and share information, streamlining operations and improving efficiency.

How do I navigate between different modules?

You can navigate between modules using the main navigation menu at the top of the screen. Click on the module name to access it. You can also use the sidebar navigation within each module to access specific features and functions. Additionally, you can use the search function to quickly find specific features or information.

Can I customize my dashboard?

Yes, you can customize your dashboard to display the information most relevant to your role. To do this:

  1. Click on the "Customize" button in the top-right corner of your dashboard
  2. Drag and drop widgets to rearrange them
  3. Click the "+" button to add new widgets
  4. Click the "x" on a widget to remove it
  5. Click "Save" when you're done

Your customized dashboard will be saved for future sessions.

Account & Access Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click on "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password

If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact your system administrator.

How do I change my user profile information?

To update your user profile:

  1. Click on your profile picture or initials in the top-right corner of the screen
  2. Select "My Profile" from the dropdown menu
  3. Click "Edit Profile"
  4. Update your information as needed
  5. Click "Save Changes"

What should I do if I'm locked out of my account?

If you're locked out of your account due to too many failed login attempts:

  1. Wait 30 minutes for the account to automatically unlock
  2. If you still can't access your account after 30 minutes, contact your system administrator

For security reasons, only system administrators can manually unlock accounts.

Finance Module Questions

How do I create a new invoice?

To create a new invoice:

  1. Navigate to Finance > Accounts Receivable > Invoices
  2. Click New Invoice
  3. Select a customer from the dropdown
  4. Fill in the invoice details (date, due date, reference number, etc.)
  5. Add line items by clicking Add Item
  6. Review the invoice total
  7. Click Save to save as draft or Post to finalize the invoice

For more detailed instructions, see the Invoicing section in the Finance Module documentation.

How do I reconcile bank statements?

To reconcile bank statements:

  1. Navigate to Finance > Banking > Bank Reconciliation
  2. Select the bank account to reconcile
  3. Enter the statement date and ending balance
  4. Match transactions in the system with those on your bank statement
  5. Click Reconcile when all transactions are matched and the difference is zero

For more detailed instructions, see the Banking section in the Finance Module documentation.
